The Program

What CIAP Is

CIAP is not a random influencer course. It is a purpose-built civic communications accelerator training serious digital voices for Nigeria’s future.

The Civic Influencer Accelerator Program (CIAP) is a TMN-powered initiative designed to train, equip, and launch the next generation of disciplined Nigerian civic influencers. Whether you are already creating content or just starting, CIAP gives you the tools, the knowledge, and the community to do it seriously.

CIAP is not about chasing followers. It is about building real influence — the kind that shapes conversations, earns money, and moves Nigeria forward.

The Movement Nigeria is backing and paying for this 3-month program. You only cover a small registration fee. The course, the mentorship, the community — all of it is backed and paid by TMN.

Why Join

Why CIAP Exists

Nigeria has millions of content creators. But most are creating without direction — chasing trends, struggling to monetise, and making zero lasting impact.

CIAP exists because The Movement Nigeria believes that influence is a skill — and like every skill, it can be taught, structured, and improved.

If you are already an influencer, CIAP helps you go further, earn more, and build something that lasts. If you are not yet an influencer, CIAP is your structured entry point.

And because you will be part of the TMN community, you will never be building alone. Every piece of content you create will have a ready community behind it — engaging, sharing, and supporting your voice from day one.

The Curriculum

What You Will Learn

Eight powerful learning areas across 3 months — all designed to make you a credible, confident, and earning civic influencer.

01

Civic Storytelling

How to explain public issues — governance, policy, and national affairs — in language everyday Nigerians understand and care about.

02

Niche Selection & Expansion

How to choose the right niche for your voice, own it completely, and expand from it into broader influence over time.

03

Content Creation Skills

Short-form and long-form content — hooks, scripts, captions, video structure, comment management, and platform-specific strategies.

04

Audience Development

How to build real, loyal audiences based on trust and consistency — not empty virality or bought followers that disappear.

05

Monetisation Strategies

How to turn your influence into income — brand deals, community monetisation, digital products, and sustainable earning as a creator.

06

Fact-Checking & Responsibility

How to verify information before publishing, avoid misinformation, and build a reputation for accuracy and credibility.

07

Digital Courage & Ethics

How to speak boldly on important issues without crossing into recklessness, defamation, or content that damages your brand.

08

Community Building

How to build and lead your own engaged community — using TMN’s national network as your personal launchpad from day one.
