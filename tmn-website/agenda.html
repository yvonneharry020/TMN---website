Strategic Roadmap

Our National
Agenda

The Movement Nigeria operates through a phased, long-term strategic roadmap — building from immediate visibility and mobilization to lasting institutional power and national influence.

The Three Phases

Phased National
Strategy

We do not build for the next news cycle. We build for the next generation. Each phase builds on the last to create durable, scalable civic power.

Phase One

2026 – 2027
Foundation

→Establish national visibility and brand credibility
→Launch member registration and onboarding
→Build state-level coordinator networks
→Train first cohort of Civic Influencers
→Launch WhatsApp community networks
→Participate in 2027 election conversations
Phase Two

Post-2027
Consolidation

→Strengthen grassroots structures in all states
→Expand leadership development programs
→Establish policy research institution
→Build academic and industry partnerships
→Scale Civic Influencer cohorts nationally
→Deepen diaspora engagement networks
Phase Three

2031 & Beyond
Institution

→Build sustainable civic institutions
→Strengthen democratic accountability nationwide
→Promote policy-driven governance culture
→Support responsible leadership at all levels
→Contribute to a stable, prosperous, citizen-driven Nigeria

Roadmap

Key Focus Areas

What We Are Building
Toward

🗳

Democratic Participation

Driving meaningful, informed civic participation — not just voting, but year-round engagement with governance and policy at every level.

🌱

Leadership Pipeline

Systematically identifying and developing capable, ethical leaders who can take on civic, community, and political responsibilities.

📡

National Communication Network

Building a digital communication ecosystem capable of reaching and informing millions of Nigerians with credible civic content.

🏛

Civic Institution Building

Creating permanent organizational infrastructure — membership systems, training academies, policy centers — that outlast any single election cycle.

⚖

Accountability Culture

Normalizing the expectation that leaders must be accountable, transparent, and responsive — shifting Nigeria’s civic culture from the ground up.

Right Now

2026–2027

These are the specific priorities the movement is executing on right now. Every member who joins accelerates this work.

📋

Member Registration

Building a national database of registered, organized citizens across all 36 states and the diaspora.

Civic Influencer Training

Training the first 100 national civic influencers to amplify the movement’s message and civic content.
