Frequently Asked

Common Questions

Is it free to join The Movement Nigeria?

+
Yes, joining TMN is completely free. The movement is funded by supporters and donors who believe in the mission. There is no membership fee, subscription, or required payment of any kind.

I live outside Nigeria. Can I still join?

+
Absolutely. The Movement Nigeria actively welcomes diaspora Nigerians. Select “Diaspora” when registering and we will connect you to diaspora-specific networks and engagement opportunities.

What happens after I register?

+
After registering, you will be connected to the appropriate community networks for your state or diaspora location, receive civic education content, and receive invitations to programs relevant to your areas of interest.

Is TMN affiliated with any political party?

+
The Movement Nigeria is an independent civic platform. We are not affiliated with any political party. Our focus is on civic organization, leadership development, and policy advocacy across party lines and ideological backgrounds.

How is my personal data used?

+
Your data is used solely to organize your participation and connect you to the right TMN communities. We do not sell, share, or misuse your personal information. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
