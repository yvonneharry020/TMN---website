Our Background

Why TMN Was Founded

Nigeria is one of the youngest countries in the world, with a rapidly growing population of young people eager to contribute to national progress. Yet many citizens feel excluded from meaningful participation in governance and policy-making.

Across the world, youth-led civic movements have played a decisive role in shaping democratic transformation. From the student-led civic organization Otpor, which helped catalyze democratic reforms in Eastern Europe, to civic participation organizations like YIAGA Africa that promote democratic engagement — organized citizens have consistently proven capable of influencing national direction.

The Movement Nigeria draws inspiration from these global experiences while focusing specifically on the unique realities, aspirations, and opportunities within Nigeria. We are not copying a model. We are building something designed for this soil.

“Nigeria has already produced the energy. What has been missing is organization. That is the vacuum The Movement Nigeria was built to fill.”
— TMN Founding Principle

The Problem We Solve

Public interest in Nigeria does not translate into sustained engagement. Civic energy is not effectively coordinated. Long-term institutional development remains limited. TMN changes this.

Our Approach

We emphasize continuous civic participation, leadership development, and institution-building rather than relying solely on electoral cycles or social media moments.

Our Difference

What Makes TMN Different

We are not a campaign, a protest, or a moment.
We are building a disciplined, long-term institution.

🏛

Institution First

While others build campaigns that fade, we are building permanent civic infrastructure — membership systems, training pipelines, state structures, and policy capacity that outlast any single election.

🧠

Intellectual Seriousness

TMN does not do noise. Every communication is crafted to inform, educate, and elevate civic discourse. We believe that a movement is only as credible as its ideas.

📡

Digitally Native

From WhatsApp organizing networks to civic influencer training and digital education content — TMN is built for how Nigerians actually communicate and connect today.

🌍

Truly National

Not Lagos-only. Not elite-only. Not diaspora-only. TMN is designed for Nigerians across all 36 states, all ethnic backgrounds, all socioeconomic groups, and all religions.

🔗

Coalition Building

We actively build bridges between youths and professionals, between diaspora and home, between communities and policy — because lasting change requires everyone at the table.

📈

Long-Term Thinking

Our roadmap runs through 2027, 2031, and beyond. We plan, measure, and build for decades — not for the next news cycle or election campaign.
